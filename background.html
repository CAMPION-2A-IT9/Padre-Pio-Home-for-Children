PPHC BACKGROUND AND GROUP 2A PROJECT PROPOSAL

Padre Pio Home for the Children is a non stock-nonprofit charitable institution founded by Father Domenico Laballarte, an Italian priest who was
a spiritual son of St. Padre Pio for 26 years. The institution takes care of children who were neglected and abused
by their parents or guardians. They care for these children by providing
them shelter, food, education, and psychosocial intervention.


Significance of the Project

Background of the Project

What problematic situations in the partner community
we observe is the children in the institution are malnourished,
lack good health, the lack of sponsors and donations that the
institution is receiving. It was mentioned that the institution lacks
funds to pay for the bills and to provide the needs of the children.
As Ateneans we are called to help them and find ways for them to gain the strength they need.

The Intervention(Project Proposal)

Our Project is called "The Sharers of Joy" and our project is a donation box where
people can donate food packs, school supplies, used materials,
and money for the children and institution. With this project, the institution would be able to
have the help they need and be able to continue to care for the children.

Why is the project important for the institution?

The project is important for the institution because it can benefit the institution and save the issues that they are facing. The institution is asking Ateneo students for our help and support since they want to resolve this issue that they are facing. Our group wants to make sure that the project can be able to help the institution and make sure that the institution will have enough to save and provide the necessities to the children.

Why is the project important for the Ateneo students

The project is important for the Ateneo Students because as students we want to help the children and the institution and assure the help that they need. We want to ensure a bright future for them and make sure that they are more people who care for them. As Ateneo students we are servants of God and here on Earth to help others and show each other kindness.

Why is the project important for the public in general

Why the project is important for the public, in general, is because we can be able to spread awareness to others and the institution would be able to get more help from people. If we are able to spread awareness to other people about what the institution is facing, they can be able to receive more donations and sponsorships.
